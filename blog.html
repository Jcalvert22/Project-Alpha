School history

As a 2022 graduate from Muscle shoals High School, I decided to accept a golf scholarship to Calhoun Community College. Calhoun was a great first step for my academic career. I graduated in May 2024 with an Associates of Science degree. The Summer after I graduated from Calhoun, I decided to do some job shadowing to get a better idea of my career goals. I was introduced to Software Development and decided I wanted to try it. After being in software development for a semester, I decided cybersecurity was a better fit for me. Since making this decision, I have really enjoyed my classes and feel like I am on the right path.

Certifications

During the summer, I completed my certification for CompTIA Security+. I studied for the exam through a 16 hour youtube course. I then purchased many online flashcard sets that was around 800 practice questions worth of exam questions. I took it after around two weeks of studying everyday and passed.
